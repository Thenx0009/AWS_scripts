Case Study : Amazon EC2 (Elastic Compute Cloud)

Scenario: You work for a startup that is rapidly growing its customer base. The company needs to scale its web application to handle increased traffic and ensure high availability.

Objectives:

Launch EC2 instances in an Auto Scaling group to handle variable traffic loads.
Configure the Auto Scaling group to automatically add or remove instances based on CPU utilization.
Implement a load balancer to distribute traffic across multiple EC2 instances.
Set up CloudWatch alarms to trigger scaling actions.
Ensure that your application is fault-tolerant and can withstand EC2 instance failures.
