AWS Auto-Scaling is a mechanism that automatically permits you to increase or decrease your resources to meet demand based on custom-defined metrics and thresholds.
Through Auto-scaling, it’s simple to set up application scaling for multiple resources across multiple services in minutes.

Types of Auto-Scaling:-

1)Vertical Scaling involves adding more power (CPU, RAM, storage, etc.) to an existing machine.

2)Horizonatal Scaling involves adding more machines or nodes to a system
